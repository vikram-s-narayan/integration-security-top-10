# ISF-6 Playbook: Weak Vendor Due Diligence

## Risk
An insecure vendor compromises all its customers.

## Objective
Assess vendor integration security posture before trust.

## Steps
1. Inventory – List SaaS vendors with integrations.
2. Policy – Require ISF-aligned practices in RFPs.
3. Enforce – Vendor security questionnaires.
4. Automate – Track vendor risk in GRC.
5. Review – Annual re-assessment.

## Success Criteria
All vendors scored for integration security.
